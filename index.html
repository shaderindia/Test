How to Use

  • Upload a clear photo. The page will automatically scroll down to the editor.
  • Step 1: Adjust the crop box or use Smart Auto-Crop to frame your head and shoulders correctly.
  • Step 2: Click Confirm Crop & Proceed. This permanently trims the edges to save device memory.
  • Step 3: The AI tools will appear! Click Remove Background to cleanly extract your subject.
  • Pick a background color, adjust the print layout, and download!
