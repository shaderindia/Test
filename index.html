How to Use

Create professional passport photos without any data leaving your device.

  • Step 1: Upload. Select a clear, well-lit photo of yourself facing forward.
  • Step 2: Crop. Adjust the box to tightly frame your head and top of shoulders. Click Confirm Crop to lock it in and save device memory.
  • Step 3: Background AI. Once cropped, use the Erase Background tool. Pick a compliant color (white or blue usually).
  • Step 4: Print. Adjust the paper size and gaps. Download the final template and print it at exactly 100% scale (Do not "Scale to Fit").
